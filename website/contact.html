What to Expect

Our Engagement Process

From initial conversation to production deployment — a clear, structured path to AI implementation.

Discovery Call

A focused 30-minute conversation to understand your use case, data landscape, and business objectives. We'll assess feasibility and outline a preliminary approach.

Technical Assessment

Our team evaluates your data readiness, infrastructure, and requirements. We provide a detailed scoping document covering architecture, timeline, and investment.

Proposal & Kickoff

A tailored proposal with clear deliverables, milestones, and checkpoints. Once approved, we assign a dedicated team and begin the engagement.

Build & Deploy

Iterative development with continuous validation, testing, and stakeholder feedback — culminating in production-grade deployment with monitoring and support.

FAQ

Common Questions

What industries do you work with?

+

We specialise in Health Technology, Banking, and Financial Services. Our AI expertise spans clinical decision support, patient analytics, compliance automation, risk modeling, data pipelines, RAG systems, and enterprise deployment across these sectors.

What's the typical engagement timeline?

+

Timelines vary by scope. A focused AI proof-of-concept can be delivered in 4–6 weeks. Full enterprise implementations typically run 3–6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the technical assessment phase.

Can you deploy on-premise or in air-gapped environments?

+

Yes. Our containerised architecture supports on-premise, air-gapped, hybrid cloud, and fully managed cloud deployments. We design infrastructure to meet your security and compliance requirements.

What AI models and frameworks do you work with?

+

We work across the full model landscape — from open-source foundation models (Llama, Mistral) to domain-specific architectures (BioGPT, FinGPT). Our training infrastructure supports PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX, and custom training frameworks with LoRA, QLoRA, and RLHF fine-tuning.

Do you provide AI training and education?

+

Yes. We offer enterprise AI workshops, healthcare-specific AI training, AI literacy programmes, and custom corporate training curricula. Programmes are tailored to your team's level and organisational AI strategy.
